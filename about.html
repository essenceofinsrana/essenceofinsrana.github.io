About

1. About this Website

This website is intended exclusively for informational and illustrative purposes. It contains an overview of the book, its specifications, and limited excerpts from selected pages. No part of this content constitutes or substitutes the complete book. All intellectual property rights remain with the respective rights holders.

The website is not an official publication and is not affiliated with or endorsed by the author, the publisher, INS Rana, the Indian Navy, or any government authority. Its purpose is to help readers understand the essence and structure of the book. References to the author, the book, INS Rana, Indian Navy or any other entity/ organisation/ authority are made solely for historical, educational, commemorative, and archival purposes.

2. About the Name Essence of INS Rana

Visitors may notice that the expression Essence of INS Rana is used throughout this website in different ways. Most commonly, it refers to the book Essence of INS Rana, from which this website derives its name. In some places, it may refer to the website itself. In yet other places, it is used in its ordinary sense to describe the spirit, character, traditions, experiences, and legacy associated with INS Rana and the people connected with the ship. Because of these multiple meanings, the intended reference may sometimes depend on context. Where it is not explicitly stated, readers are encouraged to interpret the meaning in a way that best fits the subject being discussed.

The website's name reflects key aspects of the book, rather than claiming authority or representation.

3. Who We Are

This website is maintained by a group of volunteers, enthusiasts, and well-wishers who share a common respect and interest in the history and legacy of INS Rana.
