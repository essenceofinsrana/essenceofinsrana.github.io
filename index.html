Book Overview

The Book reflects on the glorious history of INS Rana and the significance of being associated with the Ship.

Step aboard the legendary INS Rana and embark on a journey through time, tradition, and valour. Essence of INS Rana captures the indomitable spirit of one of India’s mightiest destroyers, blending vivid history with the proud heritage and the legacy of Maharana Pratap. Through crisp storytelling and evocative images, the author pays tribute to the ship’s resolute, resilient, and relentless character, immortalising its role in shaping India’s maritime strength.

Whether a veteran, researcher, or first-time reader, discover the ship’s glorious past, ethos, and camaraderie that unite generations. Uncover the stories, visions, and moments that define INS Rana—where history becomes identity and every sailor becomes family. This book is a living memoir for all who cherish naval tradition, courage, and esprit de corps.
